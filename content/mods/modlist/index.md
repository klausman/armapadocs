## General notes

This is an overview of the mods we use, with links to the Steam Workshop.
Note that this is not *quite* complete, since we have "mods" that are just
small fixes for interactions with other mods, and those are not listed here.
Also, mods that are just strict dependencies of what we use, like Jbad and
CUP Maps Core, are not listed.
